## ğŸ¬ Recording Tips

### Voice Recording

1. **Tone:** Enthusiastic but professional
2. **Pace:** Moderate (not too fast, not too slow)
3. **Clarity:** Enunciate clearly
4. **Energy:** Maintain energy throughout
5. **Pauses:** Brief pauses between sections

### Screen Recording

1. **Smooth Movements:** No jerky cursor movements
2. **Deliberate Actions:** Click with purpose
3. **Timing:** Match voiceover timing
4. **Highlights:** Use cursor to highlight important elements
5. **Transitions:** Smooth scrolling and page transitions

### Editing

1. **Cuts:** Remove any mistakes or pauses
2. **Timing:** Ensure exactly 3:00 or less
3. **Overlays:** Add text overlays for emphasis
4. **Music:** Low volume background music (optional)
5. **Captions:** Add captions for accessibility
